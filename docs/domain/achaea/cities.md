# City-states

Achaea's continent of Sapience is divided between six player-run city-states. Each has a distinct alignment that drives roleplay, politics, and faction-level PvP. A character belongs to exactly one city (or to none, as a rogue).

| City | Alignment / focus |
| --- | --- |
| **Ashtan** | Chaos. Seaport on the Sapphire Sea, ancient power, ruled by the Chaos Court under Imperator Glaaki. |
| **Cyrene** | Culture and craft. Founded as a refuge from the wars between Ashtan and Shallam; values decency and the rule of law; tends toward neutrality. |
| **Eleusis** | Nature. Built into and beneath the forest, in deliberate harmony with its surroundings. Druidic and Sentinel home. |
| **Hashan** | Knowledge and the occult. Pursues mastery of metaphysics through relentless study; dismissive of religious morality. |
| **Mhaldor** | Evil. Theocratic dictatorship ruled by the Tyrannus; advances Evil as a cosmic principle; rejects compassion and democracy. |
| **Targossas** | Good. Island province under the Goddess Aurora and Deucalion (the Righteous Fire); a beacon for followers of Creation. |

Cities open and close over the course of Achaea's history — Shallam (the historical Good city) fell and was replaced by Targossas, and other cities have been founded or destroyed over the years. This list reflects the current six.
