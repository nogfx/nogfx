# Achaea

[Achaea, Dreams of Divine Lands](https://www.achaea.com/) is a roleplay-heavy fantasy MUD by Iron Realms Entertainment, launched in 1997. It runs on a continent called Sapience, with around 20,000 rooms, six player-run city-states, twenty-one classes, and a balance/equilibrium combat system that the world layer in `pkg/world/achaea/` is built around.

- [`cities.md`](cities.md) — the six city-states and their alignments.
- [`classes.md`](classes.md) — the twenty-one classes and their three-skill triples.
- [`skills.md`](skills.md) — the lesson/rank progression that applies to every skill.
- [`combat.md`](combat.md) — balance, equilibrium, afflictions, defences, and the healing system.
- [`channels.md`](channels.md) — communication channels (tells, city, clan, market, etc.).

Descriptions on these pages are summaries — consult `HELP <topic>` in-game or the [Achaea wiki](https://wiki.achaea.com/) for canonical wording.
